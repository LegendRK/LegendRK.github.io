Transimian

This isn't just a game where you monkey around. Solve complex puzzles with a mysterious device granting you teleporting powers.

Project ARC

In Project ARC, you assume the role of an unnamed spy who steals corporate secrets and sells them for profit. Platform your way through high-tech security and solve complex circuit mazes to steal the documents.

AfterShock: Tactical Elecspionage

Detective Gray has suddenly gained the ability to manipulate electricity, but at a steep cost: his life. Infiltrate the local crime mafia to find a cure to your deteriorating condition, while at the same time making sure to not take anyone's life.

AfterShock Map Editor

This is an application created in Windows Forms in order to streamline the creation of levels for the game AfterShock.

React-Shawn!

It's simple: you're a man named Shawn, and you must react to things! Aim for the highest score and test your arrow pushing reflexes in this not-DDR game.

Crystal Quest

Alone in purgatory. Nope, that's just what happens when a webpage hasn't fully loaded yet. Travel through an unknown world full of monsters, wizards, big men who take your money, and crystals in this RPG completely nothing like Final Fantasy.

Where's Alaska?

What happens when you have a roommate who doesn't know where Alaska is? You make a game about it of course! Show off your geographical prowess in this simple but challenging puzzle game!
